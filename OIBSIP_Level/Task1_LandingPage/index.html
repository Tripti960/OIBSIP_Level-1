Our story

Plants sold by people who actually grow them

Verdana started in 2019 when our founder Lena — a botanist frustrated with the poor health of mass-market plants — began sourcing rare species directly from growers and posting them to friends.

Three years later, those same principles still govern every decision: source ethically, pack carefully, support endlessly.

  • ✓All plants propagated in natural light, never forced
  • ✓Soil mixes formulated per species, not one-size-fits-all
  • ✓Packaging design reviewed annually by a sustainability consultant
  • ✓1% of every order donated to rewilding projects in the UK
